Awareness notes
  • Surveillance detection relies on **behavioral patterns**, not single events.
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels wrong, verify.
  • Vary routines and routes to make hostile surveillance harder.
  • Report suspicious activity through proper channels (OSAC, DHS, local authorities).
