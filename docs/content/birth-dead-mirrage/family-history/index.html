Research notes
  • Cross-check family records across multiple platforms (FamilySearch, Ancestry, MyHeritage).
  • Use immigration & military archives for missing ancestors.
  • Grave databases (Find a Grave) often link relatives together.
  • Start broad, then refine by name variations and locations.
