Copyright protection notes
  • Use plagiarism detectors (Turnitin, Copyscape, Plagscan) for text & academic content.
  • Image rights can be monitored with Pixsy, Copytrack, and Plaghunter.
  • Register works officially at Copyright.gov or SafeCreative.org for legal protection.
  • DMCA.com and Infringement Report simplify takedown requests for stolen content.
