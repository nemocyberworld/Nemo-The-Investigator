Technique notes
  • Tor & Mullvad Browser → strongest anonymity, built for anti-fingerprinting.
  • LibreWolf & Waterfox → good daily drivers with no telemetry.
  • Brave → Chromium-based with tracker/ad blocking & Tor integration.
  • Pale Moon → lightweight & legacy extension support.
  • I2P → useful for darknet-style hidden services.
  • Ungoogled Chromium & Iridium → Chromium alternatives without Google spying.
