Technique notes
  • Export first, then delete — you can’t always reverse deletion.
  • Use DSAR/Right-to-Access where legally available (e.g., GDPR).
  • Repeat opt-outs quarterly; brokers and ad settings often reset.
  • Use a unique email alias per service for future correlation.
