Academic OSINT Guidance
  • Use treaties, constitutions, accords, and international rulings as primary OSINT references.
  • Validate information by cross-checking Wikipedia, Britannica, and peer-reviewed sources (JSTOR, SSRN, etc.).
  • Leverage indices (e.g., “Soft Power Index,” “Freedom in the World,” “WGI”) for comparative geopolitical research.
  • Combine quantitative data (World Bank, IMF, OECD, UN Data) with qualitative insights (think tanks, policy briefs).
  • Archive important findings with the Wayback Machine for long-term access and reproducibility.
