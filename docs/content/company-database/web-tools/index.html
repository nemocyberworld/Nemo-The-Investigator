Technique notes
  • Match the exact legal name and jurisdiction; watch for similarly named entities.
  • Cross-check registered address, incorporation date, and officer names across sources.
  • Grab unique IDs (D-U-N-S, LEI) and reuse them to pivot to filings and sanctions checks.
  • Use historic filings to confirm continuity after mergers, rebrands, or dissolutions.
  • For D&B, include the US state for cleaner results; for multinationals, search per country.
