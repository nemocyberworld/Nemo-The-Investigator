Usage notes
  • Best queries: CAS number, UN number, or precise IUPAC name.
  • Cross-reference multiple databases for compliance and hazard validation.
  • For workplace use: check ChemHAT, GESTIS, and NIOSH.
  • For regulatory compliance: use eChemPortal and ECHA.
